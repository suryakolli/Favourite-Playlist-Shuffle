Site Description

Target Audience:

Basically, anyone who has interest in listening to music, have playlists in spotify (not necessary). You can listen to the randomly picked songs from your favourite playlists.

Takeaways:

This site is for recretion. create an account and plug in your favourite playlists and sit back to randomize the songs playing from your favorite playlists.

Problems trying to solve:

Basically, in spotify, there is currently no way to shuffle and play the songs from more than one playlist. to achieve that functionality and also to organize and group your similar playlists at one place, easy to share among your friends, this platform is way to go.

Actions for the user:

Create an account, and login to it. it should be simple process and nothing complicated as there is no password authentication (as of now). once logged in, you are prompted to add your favourite playlist ids to save them into your account. once some playlists are added, you can refresh the page and sit back and enjoy.
